About UTRS+

UTRS+ combines automated reconciliation with intelligent tax insights to ensure both financial accuracy and tax compliance for SMEs that receive payments by banks, POS terminals and mobile apps.

Problem

SMEs receive payments across many channels. Manual reconciliation is slow and error-prone, leading to unreliable tax reporting.

Solution

UTRS+ centralizes transaction feeds, matches payments with invoices using ML, identifies tax impacts and exports reconciled data to accounting software.

Vision

To be Africa’s most trusted platform for intelligent financial reconciliation and tax accuracy.

Core Features

Real-time Dashboard

Consolidate payments from all channels into a single dashboard with live reconciliation status.

Smart Tax Assist

Flags tax-relevant transactions and generates pre-filled tax forms based on verified data.

Offline-first

Works without constant internet — syncs automatically when connected.
